Background

Architect of Intelligent Systems

Where rigorous research meets applied engineering — and where theory becomes practice in the classroom.

Simba Zengeni is a PhD Researcher in Artificial Intelligence whose work sits at the intersection of machine learning systems, digital transformation strategy, and inclusive technology design. His doctoral research interrogates how AI systems can be built not only to perform — but to perform equitably.

As a Software Engineering lecturer, he translates research-grade complexity into structured, repeatable pedagogical frameworks. He does not believe in making things easier — he believes in making students more capable.

His work spans applied AI model development, enterprise system architecture, and academic curriculum design — a profile that positions him as both a practitioner and a scholar.
